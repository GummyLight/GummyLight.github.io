Current Focus

I am currently a master's student at the DEFENSE Group, PALM Lab, the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, under the supervision of Professor Min-ling Zhang and Professor Shimin Di.

My research interests primarily lie in embodied intelligence: building agents that can understand environments, learn from interaction, and make decisions grounded in perception and action.

Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Southeast University.

This website is where I collect reading notes, experiment logs, competition reflections, and practical engineering notes.
