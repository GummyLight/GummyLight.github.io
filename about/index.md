Hi, I am Changjin Li, currently a Master's student at the [DEFENSE Group](https://sdiaa.tech/), [PALM Lab](https://palm.seu.edu.cn/), the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, under the supervision of Professor [Min-ling Zhang](https://palm.seu.edu.cn/zhangml/) and Professor [Shimin Di](https://sdiaa.github.io/).

My research interests primarily lie in embodied intelligence. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Southeast University.

I use this website to organize course notes, research training, competition experience, reading notes, and technical setup records into a form that is easier to revisit and reuse.
